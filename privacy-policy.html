🛡️ Our Core Promise

🔒 Reply Snap is a 100% offline application. Your data never leaves your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information to any server, cloud service, or third party.

🚫 Information We Do NOT Collect

Reply Snap does not collect any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or network-based)
  • Contacts, call logs, or SMS messages
  • Photos, videos, or files beyond the user-selected screenshot
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, serial number)

  • Health, fitness, or medical data
  • User account credentials or login tokens

⚙️ How the App Works

Reply Snap allows users to take or select a screenshot of a chat conversation, extract the text using on-device Optical Character Recognition (OCR), and generate contextual reply suggestions — all processed entirely on the user's device.

Process Where It Happens Data Sent to Server?
Screenshot text extraction (OCR) On-device (Google ML Kit) ❌ No
Reply generation On-device (local templates) ❌ No
Reply history storage On-device (Hive local database) ❌ No
Favorites On-device (Hive local database) ❌ No
Language & tone preferences On-device (Hive local database) ❌ No

🔐 Personally Identifiable Information (PII) Protection

When text is extracted from screenshots, the App automatically detects and redacts sensitive information before storing anything in the local reply history. This includes:

  • Email addresses (e.g., user@example.com → [EMAIL REDACTED])
  • Phone numbers — Bangladesh (+880) and India (+91) formats
  • OTP codes, PIN numbers, and verification codes

✅ This redaction happens entirely on your device. No PII is ever stored in the reply history database, even locally.

📺 Advertisements

Reply Snap displays advertisements through Google AdMob to support the free availability of the App. These ads may include:

  • Banner Ads: displayed on the home screen
  • Rewarded Video Ads: shown when the user has reached the free usage limit (every 3 generations)

Users are never forced to watch ads. If an ad fails to load or the device is offline, the App continues to function normally without blocking access.

🔑 App Permissions

Permission Why It's Needed

🗄️ Data Retention & Deletion

All data generated by Reply Snap (reply history, favorites, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using an encrypted local database (Hive).

  • Reply history is automatically capped at the 20 most recent entries
  • Users can manually delete all history at any time from the Settings screen
  • Uninstalling the App permanently and irrecoverably deletes all locally stored data
  • No backups of your data are stored on any server or cloud service

👶 Children's Privacy

Reply Snap is rated for Everyone and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of personal data input. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through a third-party service integrated in the App, please contact us.

🔒 Security Measures

  • All local data is stored in an encrypted Hive database on the device
  • The release build uses R8 code obfuscation and Proguard rules to prevent reverse engineering
  • PII is automatically redacted before any local storage operation
  • No API keys, tokens, or user credentials are stored in the application
  • The App is signed with a secure 2048-bit RSA release key
